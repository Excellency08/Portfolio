Our Mission: Quality, Every Time.

Since 1952, The Colonel's Kitchen has been committed to serving fresh, hand-breaded chicken using the finest ingredients and our secret blend of spices.

A Tradition of Taste

The Colonel's Kitchen began in a small roadside diner, founded by Colonel Sanders in the 1950s. His tireless dedication to perfection and his belief in "making things the right way" still guides every restaurant today.

We continue to use only premium cuts of chicken, sourced from trusted farms, and prepared fresh daily in every store. It's more than a meal; it's a legacy.
